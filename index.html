Welcome to AH LogBook Entries tool!

This tool is meant to help you count your flying hours and fill out your personal loogbook according to the suggested format by DACM. It uses real hours extracted from E-Crew platform as well as Manex sunrise and senset hours alongside with Manex block hours if selected.

This tool is in it's Alpha version, it relies heavily on your feedback to get it stable, please report back any mulfunctions or bugs in order to improve it. You can always mail me on bk_boukraa@esi.dz  or call me on 0770703517 for any further suggestions.

This tool does not provide any guarantee for the correct totals, so it's suggested to check and crosscheck any output.

This tool may perform quicker with FOs compared to Captains due to the lack of FOs names on the logbook entries section of E-Crew for Captains (it shows self instead) which triggers an extra process in order to lookup missing names.
